Reccomended Dishes

Sisigsilog

It's hard to go wrong with one of best staple dishes in the Philippines. Derived from the dishes "sisig" and "itlog", those two freshly cooked along with a cup of Tapsimoto's signature yellow rice are what this dish is served with, all for a modest price!

Grilled Liempo

Liempo grilled like no other, along with a freshly fried egg, and calamansi and chili if you prefer your pork with a little extra flavor on the sour or spicy side! Great for days if you're feeling particularly celebratory and wanna express that through a nice delicious meal.

Tapsilog

The old classic breakfast meal for Filipinos. Served with freshly cooked beef tapa, a newly fried egg, and full Filipino authenticity, and your mood can only get better from there! Best eaten as a breakfast meal to give yourself a great start to a long day ahead.
